📅4-Week Campaign Plan + Personal Outreach

Team Commitment:

Each person posts 3x/week + personally invites 20 specific people

Week 1: Personal Stories + Lead Capture

  • Share why The Arc matters to you
  • Include "Get event updates" link for people not ready to buy
  • Focus on connection, not selling
  • Personal task: Identify your 20 people to personally invite

Week 2: Show the Impact + Soft Follow-up

  • Highlight Arc programs and services
  • Emphasize event value (food, games, prizes)
  • Follow up with people who signed up for updates
  • Personal task: Make 5 phone calls to top prospects

Week 3: Create Urgency + Direct Asks

  • "Only 2 weeks left!" messaging
  • Push referral program hard
  • "First 50 tickets get bonus raffle entries"
  • Personal task: Personal text/call to remaining 15 people

Week 4: Final Push + Last Chance

  • Last chance messaging
  • Share excitement for event
  • "Only X tickets left" scarcity messaging
  • Personal task: Final follow-up with warm leads who haven't bought

Posting Requirements:

Schedule: 7-9am, 11am-1pm, or 6-8pm
Required: Event date, ticket link, @TheArcOfDFW, #PuttsForProgress
