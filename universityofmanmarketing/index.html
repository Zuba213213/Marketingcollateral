Business Model Snapshot
What Jordan is selling and how the revenue works
Entity
UMAN Global, LLC. Austin, TX. Founded by Jordan Bowditch. 15+ years in men's work. Co-runs Sex & Love Co. with wife Alexa.
Core Product
The Mandate Summit ATX — 4-day residential men's retreat in Dripping Springs TX. $2,997 public price / $1,500 early commitment. 30-spot cap.
Revenue Model
Event tickets + private 1:1 coaching. No visible recurring digital product, course, or membership. Revenue is event-dependent and lumpy.
Pricing Reality
Public page shows $2,997. FanBasis checkout reveals a $1,500 early-commitment tier plus a $1,000 lodging add-on — two separate platforms, no consolidation.
02
Ideal Client Profile
Reconstructed from copy, testimonials, speaker lineup, and brand voice
Demographics
Men, mid-20s to mid-40s. Likely single, recently divorced, or relationally unfulfilled. Based in or willing to travel to Austin-adjacent US markets. Has disposable income — the price point self-selects.
Psychographics
"Nice guys" who've been successful-ish but feel something is missing. Latent emotional intelligence, underdeveloped. Open to somatic/breathwork but allergic to overtly woo framing. Gym-going, podcast-listening, self-improvement-adjacent.
Core Pain
Isolated. Over-relies on romantic partner for emotional needs. Lacks male friendship depth. Successful on paper, hollow in practice.

"Playfully Sincere Brotherhood" is genuine differentiation — it separates Jordan from the hyper-masculine Andrew Tate lane AND the overly earnest men's therapy lane. He occupies a real third space that few in the market have successfully claimed.

05
Visible Gaps
Where the business is losing money and momentum right now
No content funnel
Social content exists but there's no systematic path from cold follower to warm lead to event buyer. The homepage captures email with zero value exchange — no lead magnet, no welcome sequence. Jordan's personality is his best asset and it's trapped on Instagram.
Revenue is a single cliff
One or two events per year at 30 seats. At the real blended price (~$1,500–$2,997/seat), gross revenue per event is likely $60–90K before venue, food, speakers, and logistics. One bad sales cycle and the business stalls completely.
No acquisition mechanism
No visible paid traffic, no SEO content, no referral system, no affiliate network — despite having a speaker lineup full of operators who could be sending warm leads systematically.
Platform fragmentation
ClickFunnels for the main site. FanBasis for early-commitment checkout. Telegram for community. No CRM connecting them. Jordan is likely managing outreach manually across all three. This is a tax on his time and a leak in the conversion funnel.
No AI or automation infrastructure
Zero evidence of lead qualification automation, call booking flows, or post-event follow-up sequences. Every handoff is manual — a massive time drain that caps scale at Jordan's personal bandwidth.
No off-season revenue
No digital product, no membership, no group program. Revenue stops when events stop. The community he's building has nowhere to live between gatherings.

01
Build the Foundation
Create the infrastructure that turns strangers into leads

The customer doesn't know Jordan yet. He may find him through a Reel, a friend's mention, or a search. What he finds next either captures him or loses him.

#Customer StageWhat HappensDeliverable
M1Discovers JordanA man sees Jordan's content — a Reel, a share, a mention — and something resonates. He visits the website for the first time.Homepage rebuild: story, origin, social proof, and a genuine lead magnet ("The Brotherhood Assessment" opt-in).
M2First Impression LandsHe reads who Jordan is, recognizes his own pain in the language, and understands immediately what University of Man is about.Clear one-page message hierarchy: Problem → Solution → Who it's for → What to do next. No confusion.

02
Optimize the Experience
Make every touchpoint feel guided, trustworthy, and earned

The lead is warm but not yet committed. He knows the voice. Now he needs to be moved from interest to action — with zero friction and full confidence.

#Customer StageWhat HappensDeliverable
M4Wants to Know MoreHe's been through the email sequence and starts engaging. He opens emails, clicks links, maybe replies. Curious but still evaluating.AI intake agent (SMS/call) activates: answers FAQs, surfaces objections, books a discovery conversation automatically.
M5The Conversation HappensHe gets on a call — or receives a structured message sequence — that meets him where he is. He feels heard, not sold to.Discovery call framework + qualification rubric. A consistent, low-pressure conversation Jordan (or a future team member) can run.

Phase 2 outcome: A warm lead becomes a paying attendee who is already primed for transformation before he walks through the door.
03
Activate the Framework
Build the systems that compound — retention, referral, recurring revenue

The event is over — and that's where most coaching businesses lose the plot. Phase 3 is what turns a one-time attendee into a lifelong community member and a recurring revenue contributor.

#Customer StageWhat HappensDeliverable
M7The Event Changes HimHe attends The Mandate Summit. The experience delivers on the promise. He's connected to brothers, cracked open, and hungry for more.Post-event integration sequence (3 emails over 2 weeks). Reflection prompts, community reinforcement, next-step offer.
M8He Stays ConnectedHe stays active in Telegram, opens Jordan's emails, follows the content — and begins telling other men about it.Referral system: structured ask, shareable assets, incentive structure. Speaker affiliate pipeline operational.
M9He Becomes a MemberHe joins the ongoing digital community or group program. He's now a recurring contributor — not just an event attendee.Digital product launch: monthly group calls, course, or brotherhood membership. Off-season revenue stream established.
Phase 3 outcome: A one-time event buyer becomes a community member, a referral source, and a recurring revenue contributor — with no additional acquisition cost.
